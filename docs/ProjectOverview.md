# AutoCare Service Center Management System

## Project Overview

AutoCare is a full-stack Automobile Service Center Management System designed to simplify and modernize the vehicle servicing process. It enables customers to track their vehicle's service progress in real time while allowing service center employees and managers to efficiently manage service requests, estimates, approvals, updates, and billing.

## Objectives

- Provide real-time service updates to customers.
- Reduce communication gaps between customers and service centers.
- Implement role-based access for customers, employees, and managers.
- Allow customers to approve or reject repair estimates online.
- Maintain complete digital service history for every vehicle.
- Generate invoices after service completion.
- Recommend future maintenance using AI-based analysis.
